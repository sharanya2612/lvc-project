Need Support?

If you're facing any issues, our support team is just a click away. You can reach out to us through chat, email, or phone.

To join a live class, simply log in to your account, navigate to the "Live Classes" section, and click on the "Join" button next to the class you want to attend. Make sure your device has a stable internet connection.

If your microphone isn't working, first check that it is not muted in your browser or operating system settings. Ensure the correct microphone is selected in the settings of the virtual class platform. If the issue persists, try restarting the platform or your device.

After each live class, the recording will be available in the "Class Recordings" section of the platform. You can access the recording by clicking on the class you want to watch and clicking "View Recording".
